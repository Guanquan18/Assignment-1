Premium CFR finish

CFR stands for the best of the best - both in terms of performance and optics. With special decals, dynamic CFR branding, and a premium finish only found on CFR models, this bike is unmatched by anything else out there on the course.

Aerodynamic seatstays

With their wing-like profile, the seatstays of the Speedmax don't just look the part, they are also super aero thanks to their CFD-optimised design. And with their premium CFR branding, there's no mistaking that this frame is the very best that Canyon have to offer.

Fully UCI approved and WorldTour proven

Geometry, tube dimensions, length-to-width ratio: this bike pushes the absolute limits of aero design all while remaining fully within the UCI regulations. World Tour-proven, this is the bike our pros choose when it's time to fight the clock.

Adjustable cockpit

Specially made for time trialling: the Speedmax CFR TT combines a Canyon HB0058 Basebar Drop CF with an aero V19 aluminium stem. The design lets you dial in your perfect TT position using a range of different spacers and extensions. New premium Zipp Vuka Shift extensions have smart bar-end shifters that eliminate the need for an additional blip box.e valve.
